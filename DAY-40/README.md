📦 Packaging Company Data Analysis (SQL Project)
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing production, defect rates, and sales performance for a packaging company. The goal is to generate actionable insights that improve operational efficiency, product quality, and revenue growth.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze production efficiency
Identify defect rates and quality issues
Evaluate revenue and profitability
Track monthly production trends
Detect unsold inventory
🗂️ Database Schema
1. Products Table
Stores product details such as name, category, and cost per unit.
2. Production Table
Contains daily production data including quantity produced and defects.
3. Sales Table
Tracks product sales, quantities sold, and selling price.
📊 Key Business Insights
📉 Defect Rate Analysis
Calculated defect percentage for each product to monitor quality.
💰 Revenue Analysis
Identified top-performing products based on total revenue.
📅 Monthly Production Trend
Analyzed production volume across different months.
📦 Category-wise Profit
Compared revenue, cost, and profit across product categories.
⚠️ High Defect Days
Detected days where defect rate exceeded 5%.
📈 Running Total Revenue
Calculated cumulative revenue over time using window functions.
🏆 Best Category by Profit Margin
Identified the most profitable category.
📉 Unsold Inventory
Found products where production exceeded sales.
